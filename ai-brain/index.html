The Founder AI Brain Framework

Three pillars, installed properly, in two to four weeks. This is the method behind every AI Brain engagement.

01 · Context flows in automatically

Your knowledge store (Notion, Google Drive, or Obsidian) connects directly to your AI. Documents, briefs, decks, and notes become available without you uploading them. The hour a day you currently spend re-pasting context disappears.

02 · Workflows run as systems, not prompts

The three to five jobs you do every week become tuned project workspaces with reusable skills. Brand briefs, investor updates, customer replies, campaign strategy. One click, not one hour. They get better over time, not worse.

03 · Archetypes for the roles you haven't hired

Written definitions of the AI colleagues your business needs. The sales development rep, the operations manager, the analyst. Each one with a clear job description, escalation rules, and quality bar. Your next hire starts with a system that already works.
